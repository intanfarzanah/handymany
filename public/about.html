About HandyMany

A dedicated platform for booking trusted handyman services, connecting users with skilled professionals for tasks such as plumbing, electrical work, and general home repairs. By partnering with independent service providers, KPTM, and TVET students, the platform ensures access to a wide pool of qualified talent.

This platform not only delivers efficient and convenient home solutions to users but also supports local partnerships by creating accessible work opportunities for skilled individuals, contributing to community growth and professional development.

What Makes HandyMany Special?

Exclusive Institutional Partnership

We collaborate with KPTM and TVET institutions, giving access to a certified, skilled talent pool that competitors cannot easily tap into.

Student-Led Initiative

We benefits from a student-led team that brings fresh perspectives, adaptability, and a strong drive for innovation, allowing the platform to evolve quickly and respond to challenges with creative solutions.

Social impact focus

We create job opportunities for students builds community trust and brand loyalty.

Verified Provider Trust

We ensure service reliability by featuring providers verified through trusted institutions like KPTM, creating a built-in layer of customer confidence.
